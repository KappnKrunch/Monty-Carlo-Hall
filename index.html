The Monty Hall Problem

We can assume that we will have the same prbability regardless of which door we choose initially. For this reason we will say that we have choosen door #1. We label the case where the car is behind door n as Hn, and call D the case where monty opens door #3 and offers a switch to door #2.


We're looking for the likliehood that the car is behind door #n given that we selected the first door, monty opened door #3, and we were offered a switch to door #2.
